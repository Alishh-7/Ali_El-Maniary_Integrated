At our spa, we offer six treatment rooms for your relaxation and rejuvenation. Upon arrival, we recommend wearing loose, comfortable clothing for facials. For body molding and body wraps, you’ll simply wear your robe and undergarments and can change in the treatment room. Appointments are available from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm and can accommodate up to two people at a time, subject to accommodation size and availability of our spa practitioners. Please note our cancellation policy: 100% of the full treatment price will be charged for cancellations and no-shows made less than twelve (12) hours prior to the appointment time.

To ensure a successful Algonquin spa experience, we encourage you to pre-book your appointment. While we strive to accommodate all bookings, we may need to reschedule due to unforeseen circumstances. Rest assured, we’ll notify you promptly and work with you to find an alternative. Treatments will be charged to your account with a 13% HST added. We kindly ask that you observe our no extra tipping policy. Instead, consider booking another appointment, engaging in another activity, enjoying another glass of wine, or extending your stay—all of which contribute to enhancing your overall experience.
